About YES Securities (India) Limited

Company Overview

Established in 2013, YES Securities (India) Limited is a wholly-owned subsidiary of YES Bank Limited. We are a full-service financial services firm offering retail and institutional stock broking, wealth management, and investment advisory.

With 400–500 professionals across Mumbai (HQ), Delhi, Bengaluru, and regional branches, we serve hundreds of thousands of clients across India, leveraging YES Bank's extensive branch network.

Regulatory & Financial Strength

  • • SEBI-registered broker (NSE/BSE member, CDSL & NSDL depository participant) and investment advisor.

  • • ₹290 crore working capital facility to support operations and growth.
  • • CDSL & NSDL depository participant for secure custody of assets.
